About the Conference

The International Conference on Sustainable Energy and Intelligent Systems (ICSEIS'25) is a premier gathering of researchers, engineers, and industry professionals dedicated to advancing sustainable energy solutions and intelligent system technologies. This conference serves as a platform to exchange cutting-edge ideas, explore innovative methodologies, and foster collaboration among experts from academia and industry. With a focus on renewable energy, smart grids, energy-efficient systems, AI-driven automation, and embedded intelligent technologies, ICSEIS'25 aims to address global challenges related to energy sustainability and digital transformation. The event will feature keynote speeches from renowned experts, technical sessions, and networking opportunities to inspire groundbreaking discussions and future innovations. Join us to contribute to the future of sustainable and intelligent technologies that will shape the next generation of energy solutions.

Organizer

The Conference will be organised by the Laboratory of Intelligent Systems, Materials, and Sustainable Energy (SIMED), faculty of sciences and technics, Moulay Ismail University of Meknes (UMI)-Morocco.

Authors Guidelines

  • All submissions must be in English.
  • Each paper will be reviewed by three members of the scientific committee.
  • All submitted papers must be original and not published or presented elsewhere.
  • Manuscripts must follow the following SPRINGER template, including figures, tables and references.
  • Only electronic submissions in PDF format will be considered.
  • All papers must be submitted via the ICSEIS 2025 Microsoft CMT online submission website.
  • No changes can be made during the review process. If accepted, minor revisions may be made before final submission.

Format of the event

The International Conference on Sustainable Energy and Intelligent Systems will feature a dynamic and interactive format designed to foster knowledge exchange, collaboration, and innovation. The event will include:

  • Keynote Speeches: Renowned experts from academia, industry, and research institutions will share insights on emerging trends and advancements in sustainable energy and intelligent systems.
  • Technical Sessions: Presentations of high-quality scientific papers covering cutting-edge research and applications in the field.
  • Panel Discussions: Interactive debates with experts and stakeholders addressing critical challenges and future directions.
  • Poster Sessions: A dedicated platform for researchers to present their work in an engaging and interactive format, encouraging discussion and feedback.
  • Networking Opportunities: Dedicated sessions to facilitate collaborations between researchers, industry professionals, and policymakers.
